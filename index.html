Hey All!

My name is Cian, I’m from Cork, but living in Limerick.

I graduated from Fine art Painting in 2018 from Limerick School of Art and Design. There I developed a strong interest in philosophy and writing and a love for making things.

I have worked as a barista in speciality coffee here in Limerick since graduating, even competing a number of times.

I was previously a member of Spacecraft Artists’ Studios and I still maintain a writing and fine art practice.

I am most excited to augment my artistic skills to a user centred design framework.
